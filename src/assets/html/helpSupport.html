About EQ8

EQ8DAO is a decentralized autonomous organization that is building the world's first web3.0 DataVerse. This protocol enables secure aggregation, licensing, exchange and monetization of data that is verifiable on-chain.

The protocol uses a native utility token, EQ8, for staking based UGC (user generated content), research, promotions and governance. One of the initiative's primary aim is to enable gamified data aggregation, and fractionalized ownership of dNFTs (Data-as-NFTs).

EQ8DAO would generate revenue by charging a platform fee in EQ8 tokens primarily from data aggregation, licensing and consensual exchange for:

a. Market Research Data collected via Surveys, Polls, reviews, ratings, skill assessments

b. Skill-based Games and DeFi Contests i.e. Quiz, Trivia, Prediction Markets, etc.

c. IOT Data from smart devices and IOT sensors

The fee thus collected would be used by the EQ8DAO treasury for rewards & royalty payouts to content publishers and data providers for their time, knowledge and attention each time their dNFT is licensed to data requesters. The data requesters on the other hand would pay for genuine and verifiable data (dNFTs), that is available in near real-time.

You can learn more about EQ8 Network and its offerings by visiting www.eq8.network.


 

The EQ8 App

EQ8 is a decentralized app with a non-custodial multi-chain wallet that lets Data Requestors, Data Providers and Content Creators mutually benefit from consensual data exchange via their participation in different campaigns. Data Providers/Content Creators can earn rewards for sharing their time, knowledge and attention on the EQ8 app, while Data Requestors benefit from getting authentic and verifiable data from actual Data Providers.

EQ8 App allows you to:

  • Earn Rewards - Receive Crypto Assets for participating in activities like Profile Surveys, Premium Surveys, Polls, QNFTs and referring friends.

  • Create Content – Create Questions (QNFTs - Questions as NFTs) and earn Royalty rewards each time your QNFT is used in a campaign (surveys and polls).

  • Send, receive and store Crypto Assets.

  • Manage your Identity and Data – Get complete ownership of your data as it is only shared with your consent.


 

The EQ8 Token

EQ8 Token is a utility and governance token (ERC-20) for the EQ8 DataVerse. The primary utility of EQ8 Token is to facilitate user participation in paid campaigns/competitions, user rewards and payment of subscription and usage fee for enterprises.

EQ8 Token holders will be entitled to voting rights to govern the EQ8DAO (Decentralized Autonomous Organization).


 

The EXP Token

EXP Tokens (NTT ERC 1298) are non-transferable tokens that are used to measure the Reputation Score of users. Users are rewarded with EXP points with each participation in any campaign on the EQ8 Network App. These EXP points are redeemable into rewards (crypto assets) at pre-defined milestones.

EQ8 Wallet Management

1. How to create EQ8 ID Wallet?

  • A Unique EQ8 wallet will be created every time a new user registers on the EQ8 Mobile app.

  • The EQ8 ID Wallet needs to be connected with a valid phone number. One phone number can only be associated with a single EQ8 account.

  • Users will be shown a random mnemonic phrase which they will need to remember in order to access the Wallet. Users can copy and save the mnemonic phrase in a safe location.

2. Wallet Backup

EQ8 users are requested to backup and store their mnemonic phrase and private key for Wallet security and recovery purpose.

  • A mnemonic phrase, or a passphrase, is used to generate your private key. It was first proposed by BIP39 to help users remember complicated private key strings. Mnemonic phrases are typically a collection of 12, 15, 18, or 21 words. These words are all taken from a fixed collection, and the generation order is also based on an algorithm.

  • Storing your mnemonic phrase safely and securely is of utmost importance because If someone gets access to your mnemonic phrase, they can get access to your wallet and have full control over it and all the digital assets in your EQ8 wallet.

  • After creating your wallet, you must backup your mnemonic phrase and private key. You would require the mnemonic phrase and private key to access or recover your wallet in case you change your device or your device gets stolen.


 

Rules Governing Earning Activities in EQ8 App.

QNFTs

QNFT is the abbreviation for Question-as-NFT. Each QNFT is unique and enables users to have ownership over their content/ questions and makes them entitled to earn royalty in case their content / question is used in any campaign(s).

A QNFT can be a text question or a question with media (Image, Audio, Video, etc.). Any user can mint QNFT by paying the transaction fee. The QNFT thus minted shall be put up for approval before it is added to the EQ8 question bank.

Platform users can use questions from the EQ8 question bank for conducting Surveys, Polls, Quiz, Reviews, Ratings, etc. and the QNFT owners shall receive royalty in EQ8 tokens (post EQ8 token launch) per QNFT per use.

In addition to the royalty, users shall also get experience points i.e., EXP tokens (Non-Transferable ERC-1298 Tokens) which are used to calculate reputation-based ranking of users. A user’s reputation entitles them to earn bonus or have multiplier effect on their earnings. Users will also be eligible to claim EQ8 tokens and/or other goodies upon reaching certain milestones (e.g., 2,500 EXP, 5,000 EXP, 10,000 EXP).

Other Details:

• QNFT creator shall receive 50 EXP points for each QNFT added to the Question Bank upon approval.

• QNFT creator shall receive 100 EXP points every time their QNFT is used in a campaign.

• QNFT creators shall be entitled to receive 30% of the platform fee of the campaign in which their QNFT is used as Royalty in EQ8 tokens.

• EQ8 Identity verification is mandatory for redeeming reward/royalty amount over $10 worth of value in EQ8 tokens.

• At present no collateral is required to mint QNFTs, however the creators will need to pay for the Polygon Transaction Fee (in MATIC tokens) towards minting QNFT.

• QNFTs will be approved based on pre-defined criteria such as usefulness of the questions, appropriate language, grammatically correct etc. QNFTs containing abusive, religiously sensitive or explicit content (words or media) will be rejected and creators of such content will be penalized by forfeiting their collaterals and may be blocked for repeated offense.

Reward Payout

  • EQ8 Identity verification is mandatory for redeeming the EQ8 Tokens/EXP Tokens earned as rewards through the Wallet.

  • The EXP Tokens will be awarded immediately after the QNFT is created.

  • The applicable EQ8 Tokens for QNFT usage will be awarded once in every 30 days.

Staking

  • QNFT creators can stake 1000 EQ8 Tokens and have their QNFTs listed in the premium category that may result in more usage of their QNFTs.

  • Lock-in period for the staked EQ8 Tokens will be 30 days.

Penalty

  • None


 

Profile Surveys

Profile surveys are short surveys that are used to collect key demographic information about the users (e.g., gender, age, occupation, interests etc.). The information collected through these surveys is used to:

  1. Accurately determine the target panel per the data requester need.

  2. Ensure that the users get appropriate notifications that match with their profile and interests.

  3. Improve user experience by providing them an idea of how the paid surveys work.

  4. Collect basic demographic information about the users so that they do not have to share the same information repeatedly.

Profile Survey Rules

General

  • Users/Panelists can participate in profile surveys listed on the EQ8 Mobile App in the Profile Surveys card on the Earn Screen and earn EQ8 Tokens/EXP Tokens as rewards.

  • Users will receive mobile notifications to participate in Profile Surveys.

  • Profile Survey participation is open to all.

Reward Eligibility

  • Users/Panelists who participate in Profile Surveys will be eligible to win 2 kinds of rewards:

    • EQ8 Tokens (ERC 20)

    • EXP Tokens (NTT ERC 1298) – These will be non-transferable tokens and will not have any monetary value.

  • EQ8 Tokens (After token launch) will be awarded to only those users who successfully complete a Profile Survey and the applicable Token reward will be credited in the user’s EQ8 wallet.

  • 10 EXP Tokens will be awarded to only those users who successfully complete a Survey and the applicable Token reward will be credited in the user’s EQ8 wallet.

  • No EXP Tokens or EQ8 tokens will be awarded to the Profile Survey participants who are screened out, are quota-full or abandon the Profile Survey.

Reward Payout

  • EQ8 Identity verification is mandatory for redeeming the EQ8 Tokens earned as rewards.

Staking

  • None

Penalty

  • In case a panelist provides bad quality data, he/she will be penalized in the form of deduction of EXP points. If a user is found to be providing bad quality data multiple times, he/she maybe be subject to harsher penalties, like blacklisting, user profile blocking etc.

Reward Eligibility

  • Users/Panelists who participate in Surveys will be eligible to win 2 kinds of rewards:

    • EQ8 Tokens (ERC 20)

    • EXP Tokens (NTT ERC 1298) – These will be non-transferable tokens and will not have any monetary value.

Polls

EQ8 Network conducts Polls as they are an intrinsic part of Qualitative and Quantitative Research methods and are used to gain statistical insights to make informed decisions.

Poll Rules

General

  • Users can participate in Polls (on fulfilling eligibility criteria and confirming to the terms and conditions of the poll) and stand a chance to win EQ8/MATIC token as rewards.

  • Users will also be eligible to get 1 EXP point as participation reward and 10 EXP points if they are a part of the Winner’s pool.

  • Each User will need to pay the Participation Fee (in EQ8/Matic Token(s)) in order to be eligible for participation in a poll. In addition to this, user will also need to pay a very small transaction fee (in Matic).

  • Participation Fee shall be decided by the Poll Creator and may vary from poll to poll. Whereas the transaction fee is decided by the underlying blockchain.

Reward Eligibility

  • Users/Panelists who participate in polls will be eligible to win 2 kinds of rewards:

    • Pool Reward Tokens (Matic for now)

    • EXP Tokens (NTT ERC 1298) – These will be non-transferable tokens and will not have any monetary value.

  • Winner’s pool (Total Reward pool – platform fee) will be equally distributed between the users who are a part of the winning pool of the poll and the applicable Token reward will be credited in the user’s EQ8 wallet through a blockchain smart contract validation.

  • EXP Tokens will be awarded to all participants of the poll.

  • 1 Poll Answered = 1 EXP Token (irrespective of whether the user is a part of the winner’s pool or not)

  • 1 Poll Won = 10 EXP Tokens

Entitlement Calculation

  • For Polls, the amount of Matic Tokens awarded in the poll will depend upon the total pool size of the poll and the number of participants falling in the winning group of the poll.

  • EQ8 will charge 10% of the total Poll Reward Pool as Platform Fee.

  • For e.g., if the total Poll Reward Pool is $100, then the Platform Fee would be $10 and $90 worth of reward pool will be distributed amongst the winners.

Reward Payout

  • EQ8 Identity verification Completion is mandatory for redeeming the EQ8 Tokens (After EQ8 token launch) earned as rewards through the Wallet.

  • After poll completion, winners will have to claim the applicable Poll reward from within the app and the reward will be credited in the EQ8 wallet of respective users.

Staking

  • Users can stake EQ8 Tokens to get priority access to polls before those users who do not stake EQ8 Tokens.

  • Lock-in period for the staked EQ8 Tokens will be 30 days.

Penalty

  • None

Device fingerprinting is the process of identifying a system based on data points that are unique to it. Examples of such data include an IP address, a time zone, a browser, screen resolution, a language, and an operating system (OS). Device fingerprinting even takes into account a list of installed plug-ins and fonts in the system.
